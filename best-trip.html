📅 7-Day Outline

Day 1 (Aug 22): Arrive Naples → train to Salerno → bus to Scala (~3hr) → check in → Babel dinner in Ravello
Day 2: Bus to Amalfi → Duomo → Atrani beach → optional ferry to Positano
Day 3: 🚌 Positano full day — Spiaggia Grande, cliffside lunch, vertical streets
Day 4: ⛰️ Path of the Gods hike — Bomerano → Nocelle (3–4hr, cliff-edge), bus down to Positano
Day 5: Villa Rufolo + Villa Cimbrone morning → terrace afternoon → Ravello Festival concert?
Day 6: ⛵ Capri day trip — ferry €23, Monte Solaro chairlift, Marina Piccola swim
Day 7: Last beach at Atrani → Pansa pastries → sunset from your terrace → splurge dinner
Day 8 (Aug 29): Bus → Amalfi → Salerno → Naples → NAP flight (allow 4–5hr)
