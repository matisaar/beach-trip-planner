👉 Bottom Line

For this trip: book the Airbnb

The math is simple. A $150/night Old Town apartment gives you more space, a kitchen (saving $130+ on food), a private terrace, and character that most hotels can’t match. The only hotels that compete on location and charm cost $300+/night — which would blow the budget by $1,000+.

The one exception: if you really value daily housekeeping and included breakfast, the Belmondo ($1,400 total) or Ambassadors Residence ($1,540 total) are solid mid-range choices that don’t wreck the budget.
