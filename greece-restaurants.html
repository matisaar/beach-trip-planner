Free raki tradition: In Crete, tavernas bring free raki (grape spirit) and often a small dessert after your meal. This is a genuine Cretan tradition, not a tourist gimmick. Don’t refuse it — it’s considered rude.
Cook 3 nights: With a kitchen in your Airbnb, do taverna dinners 4 nights and cook 3 nights. Hit Agora Market for tomatoes, feta, olives, fresh bread — a €10 shop feeds two for dinner. Cretan tomatoes in August are unreal.
