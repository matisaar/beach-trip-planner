💡 Booking Strategy

When & How to Book

For August 22–29, 2026 flights from Toronto
  • Best booking window: January–March 2026. Summer Greece flights from Canada get expensive fast.
  • Set alerts: Google Flights price alerts for YYZ→CHQ and YYZ→ATH. Set both and compare.
  • Book separate legs: Sometimes YYZ→ATH on Air Transat + ATH→CHQ on Aegean is $400 cheaper than a single booking. Check the math.
  • Flexibility: If you can depart Aug 21 or 23, check ±1 day — mid-week departures are often $100–200 cheaper.
  • Aeroplan: AC direct to ATH earns Aeroplan points. Turkish is Star Alliance too. Redeem points for the ATH→CHQ hop (7,500 points one-way).
Compared to Italy: Flights to Crete cost about the same as flights to Naples ($1,400–$2,000 range). The difference is that Crete is 30–40% cheaper on the ground — food, hotels, activities. You save on the trip, not the flight.
