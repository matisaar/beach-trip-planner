💡 Booking Strategy

When & How to Book

For August 18–31, 2026 flights from Toronto
  • Prices are “typical” right now (Feb 2026). Google says “cheapest time to book is now, until ~April” for the return leg. Don’t wait too long.
  • Book 3 separate tickets: (1) YYZ→ATH, (2) ATH→CHQ on Aegean, (3) CHQ→ATH + ATH→YYZ. This gives you the best prices and flexibility.
  • Set Google Flights alerts for both YYZ→ATH and ATH→YYZ. Prices for the AC nonstop could drop to ~$800 if you catch a sale.
  • Flexibility: Check ±1 day on departure. Flying Aug 17 (Sunday) or Aug 19 (Tuesday) could save $50–150.
  • Aeroplan points: AC nonstop to ATH earns Aeroplan. Aegean is also Star Alliance. The Iberia return doesn’t earn Aeroplan (Oneworld) but is $135 cheaper than BA.
  • Travel insurance: With 3 separate tickets, if you miss the ATH→CHQ hop due to a delayed international flight, you’re rebooking yourself. That’s the trade-off. Get travel insurance.
