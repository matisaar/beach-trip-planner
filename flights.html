💡 Booking Tips

Set up price alerts: Google Flights has a “Track prices” toggle. Flip it on for both the Naples and Rome routes. You’ll get email notifications when prices drop. Kayak and Skyscanner have their own alert systems too.
Best time to book: For August 2026 flights, the sweet spot is typically 3–5 months before departure (March–May 2026). Prices are “currently typical” per Google, so they’re unlikely to drop much, but worth monitoring.
Baggage fees not included: Prices shown are base fares + taxes. Checked bag fees vary: Iberia €25–70, ITA €30–55, Air Transat includes 1 checked bag, Air Canada includes 1–2 bags on transatlantic. Always check at checkout.
Flexible date trick: Use Google Flights’ date grid to see prices for every day combination. Flying Wed or Thu can save $50–150 vs weekend departures. Our Aug 21 (Fri) shows slightly different deals than Aug 22 (Sat).
Airport taxi to Scala: Whether you land at Naples (NAP) or Rome (FCO→train to Napoli Centrale), the final leg is the same: SITA bus from Amalfi up to Scala, or a private transfer (~€130 flat for 2 from Naples). Budget about 3 hours from Naples airport to the Airbnb door.
