The Split: 5 Nights Amalfi + 7 Nights Crete

Start with the Italian cliff-side dream — Ravello, Path of the Gods, lemons, pasta. Then fly Naples → Athens → Chania and switch to Greek island mode — Balos Lagoon, Samaria Gorge, Venetian harbour sunsets, incredible seafood. Two completely different trips stitched together.

🛫 The Flight Routing

Leg 1: YYZ → Naples (Aug 22) — ~$1,400–1,800 CAD RT would become one-way ~$800–1,000
Leg 2: Naples → Athens → Chania (Aug 27) — Aegean/Volotea NAP→ATH (~€80) + Aegean ATH→CHQ (~€60) = ~$250 CAD
Leg 3: Chania → Athens → Toronto (Sept 3) — ~$800–1,000 one-way

Flight complexity: You’re booking 3 separate flight legs instead of 1 round trip. This usually costs $200–400 more total and adds transfer risk (if Leg 2 is delayed, Leg 3 doesn’t know). Build in buffer days. Book Legs 1 & 3 as a multi-city fare on Google Flights for best price.

🤔 The Verdict

If you want beaches: Pick Greece

Not even close. Balos, Elafonissi, and Seitan Limania are world-class sandy beaches with free access and crystal water. Amalfi beaches are small, rocky, and expensive. If beaches matter to you, Crete is the clear winner. Plus it’s $500 cheaper.

If you want romance + drama: Pick Italy

Nothing beats that terrace view from Windows on Heaven, Ravello at sunset, or the Path of the Gods trail. The Amalfi Coast is one of the most dramatic landscapes on Earth. The food is iconic Italian. It’s more expensive and more crowded, but the “wow” factor is higher on the coast.

+ If you can take 12+ days off: Do both

The ultimate option. Start with Italy’s cliff drama, then switch gears to Crete’s beach paradise. You get the Airbnb terrace views AND Balos Lagoon. It costs $2,500–3,000 more than either trip alone and requires 8–9 weekdays off. If the time and budget work — this is the move.

Our lean: If budget is $5K and you only have one week — go with Crete. Better beaches, better value, more variety per dollar. Italy is incredible but you can do it another year when you’re willing to spend a bit more. If you can stretch to 12 days and $8K — do both.
